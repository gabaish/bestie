Connecting Dog Owners, One Playdate at a Time

Core Features

Interactive Map

Location-based discovery of dog-friendly spots and nearby dog owners.

In-App Messaging

Direct messaging between dog owners to coordinate meetups and share experiences.

Playdate Scheduling

Easy-to-use calendar system for organizing and managing pet meetups.
